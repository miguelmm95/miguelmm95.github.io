About Me

My name is Miguel, I'm 28 years old and I was born in Valencia (Spain). Since I was a child I have been passionate about video games and as I played and growing up I began to see this medium as something more than pure entertainment and more as a medium for art and expression. After studying Microcomputer systems and networks I decided to porsue my dream of not only play videogames but also create them. I studied a Bachelor's degree of Design and Development of Video Games at Jaume I University (Castellón, Spain).

In my free time I like to play videogames alone or with my friends, do lives on twitch doing no-hit challenges, watch films and series, read sci-fi and fantasy novels and continue learning about videogame development and design.
